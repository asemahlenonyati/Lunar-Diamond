The Power Of Writing

Who is a writer?

The difficulties of being a writer

Who is a writer? A writer is a person who uses written words in various styles and techniques to communicate ideas. Being a writer means you can interpret whats happening around you and what is happening inside your brain. A writer deals with imagination and the reality of the world they live in. When you are a writer you sbould be able to connect with yourself and your audience. Who is a writer the writer can be you.

Type Of Writers

Literary and creative

There are dofferent types of writers. Writers can either be literary or creative. Literary writers deal more with what is happening around the world they live in. Creative writers del more of the imagination part this is where fiction comes in. Being a writer means you can take somone out of their world into the world you have created or the world you are showing.

Writing Styles

Different types of writing styles.

Expository Writing. The main characteristic of expository writing is that it is predominantly used to explain. Descriptive Writing. Descriptive writing as the name implies is all about description. Persuasive Writing. Narrative Writing. These are the different types of writing you get. There maybe many more other creative different types of writing. For now we will only list these few.


Lunar Diamond

About Lunar Diamond

Lunar Diamond is a platform that enables writers to network with each other. It provides networking events where writers from all over the world can come together and discuss anything to everything. This platform enables amateur writers to meet professional writers. Lunar Diamond was established in 2018 by Asemahle Nonyati who describes herself as an amateur writer. The idea of Lunar Diamonds came across as a simple idea where it was only availablet to people who write for a hobby.
